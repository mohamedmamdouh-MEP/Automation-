## Mixer Process Automation (PLC + HMI)

## OVERVIEW:
Designed and implemented an automated mixer control system using Siemens PLC and HMI. 
The project controls tank filling, mixing sequence, conveyor movement, and product discharge while providing operators with an interactive HMI for monitoring and controlling the production process.

## FEATURES:
. Automatic tank filling.

. Mixing sequence control.

. Conveyor belt operation.

. Real-time HMI visualization.

. Alarm management.

. Operator control interface.

. Production monitoring.

## TECHNOLOGIES USED:

. Siemens TIA Portal V17

. Siemens S7-1200 PLC

. HMI

. Ladder Logic
